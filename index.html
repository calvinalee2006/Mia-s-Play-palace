About

The play palace is for children and adults to take that much needed break from the reality and enjoy a day of fun filled activities! Our activities go vary from being playful and active, to discovering and learning new things in our STEM activities! We cater to private parties and birthdays, and we have new special presentations and activities every month! Come join us for a day of excitement! We will see you there!!
