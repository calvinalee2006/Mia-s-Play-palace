Mia's play palace is for children and adults to take that much needed break from the reality and enjoy a day of fun filled activities! Our activities go vary from being playful and active, to discovering and learning new things in our STEM activities! We cater to private parties and birthdays, and we have new special presentations and activities every month! Come join us for a day of excitement! We will see you there!!

Have any questions? Please feel free to contact us via e-mail or talk with us!

Activities

We have multiple ways kids can enjoy their time with at Mia's play palace. We have activities for children 2-16 and make sure each age group has something that they would enjoy and learn from. In our facility we have an arcade, a library, a theater for shows, and we have our awesome restaurant that serves the best burgers in the U.S! We want to make sure your child has fun, makes new friends in the process and leaves with great memories!

Staff

Our staff make sure that the park is always safe and clean for families to enjoy. All of our staff are first aid certified and properly trained for any emergency situation.
